Tourism in Australia – Data Visualisation

An interactive exploration of domestic tourism, economic impact, and industry trends in Australia.

Overnight Tourism Trips in Australia (2024)

States with the largest number of overnight trips are highlighted using bubble sizes. New South Wales and Victoria show the highest activity.

Tourism vs Whole Economy GDP Over Time

This chart shows the contribution of tourism GDP compared to the overall economy. The COVID-19 impact in 2020 is clearly visible.

Change in Gross Value Added by Tourism Industry

Education, transport and food services industries had the largest growth between 2022–23 and 2023–24.

Tourism Consumption Over Time

Domestic tourism consumption dominates overall tourism spend, with international consumption showing recovery after 2020.

Data sources: Tourism Research Australia, ABS
