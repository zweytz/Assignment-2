Tourism in Australia – Data Visualisation

An interactive exploration of domestic tourism, economic impact, and industry trends in Australia.

Overnight Tourism Trips in Australia (2024)

States with the largest number of overnight trips are highlighted using bubble sizes. New South Wales and Victoria show the highest activity.

Domestic Travel

Domestic tourism remains the largest contributor to overall travel activity in Australia. NSW and VIC continue to lead the nation.

International Travel

International travel has rebounded strongly since the COVID-19 downturn, with Queensland showing the largest relative growth.

Change in Gross Value Added by Tourism Industry

Education, transport and food services industries had the largest growth between 2022–23 and 2023–24.

Tourism vs Whole Economy GDP Over Time

This chart shows the contribution of tourism GDP compared to the overall economy. The COVID-19 impact in 2020 is clearly visible.

Economic Recovery

The tourism sector has recovered faster than expected, led by education, transport, and food service industries.

Key Economic Drivers

Policy changes, border reopening, and increased international arrivals are driving the rebound in tourism GDP.

Tourism Consumption Trends

Domestic tourism consumption dominates, but international consumption is recovering steadily post-pandemic.
